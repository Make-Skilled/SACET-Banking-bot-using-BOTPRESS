Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new account?

To create a new account, click on the "Register" button on the homepage. Fill in your personal details, including your name, Aadhaar number, mobile number, and email address. Once submitted, you'll receive your customer ID via email.

How can I check my account balance?

You can check your balance by logging into your account and viewing it on the dashboard. Your balance is also displayed after each transaction.

How do I transfer money?

To transfer money, go to the "Send Money" section from your dashboard. Enter the recipient's customer ID and the amount you wish to transfer. Verify the details and confirm the transaction.
